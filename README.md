# Hi, I'm Prem Pratap Singh, Ph.D.

Postdoctoral scholar at UC Davis turning multi-omics research into deployable tools for crop health. I study grapevine red blotch virus using transcriptomics, metabolomics, and digital PCR — with the goal of bridging discovery science and field-ready diagnostics.

🔗 [Website](https://prempsingh.com) · [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/prempratapsingh31) · [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.co.in/citations?hl=en&user=UGFMZEYAAAAJ) · [Email](mailto:ppssingh@ucdavis.edu)

---

### What I work on

- **Grapevine–virus interactions** — co-transcriptomics and metabolomics of GRBV-infected vines across seasons and cultivars
- **Molecular diagnostics** — RT-qPCR and digital PCR assay development for plant pathogens
- **Multi-omics data analysis** — RNA-seq pipelines, differential expression (DESeq2), GC-MS/LC-MS/MS metabolite profiling
- **Computational tools** — building R and Python workflows that make complex analyses accessible
